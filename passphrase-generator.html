Deckware

Generate a strong 79-bit passphrase

Thoroughly shuffle a partial deck of playing cards using only Ace through 6 of each of suit. Unlike Diceware, where you can decide how many words you want to generate based on your number of dice throws, this generator requires using all 24 cards to generate your passphrase. You will not generate a 79-bit passphrase until you have accounted for all 24 cards. You are strongly encouraged to not truncate your passphrase to fewer than the generated 8 words.

Random number generator

Deckware can also generate a 237-bit random number from a fully shuffled 54-card deck of playing cards. Check out the random number generator.
